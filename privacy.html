Privacy Policy

ReelBridge · Effective date: July 14, 2026

1. Overview

ReelBridge is a personal, non-commercial tool used solely by its developer. It is not a public-facing product and does not collect, store, or share data from any external users.

2. Data Collected

ReelBridge does not collect personal data from any third party. The tool processes the developer's own social media content locally on their device for the sole purpose of migrating it between platforms.

3. TikTok API Usage

ReelBridge uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload the developer's own content to their own TikTok account. The following data is accessed:

  • The developer's TikTok open_id and display name (to confirm authentication)
  • Video and image files owned by the developer

No data is stored on external servers. Access tokens are stored locally on the developer's machine only.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

ReelBridge does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

5. Data Retention

No user data is retained. The tool runs locally and does not maintain any database or remote storage.
